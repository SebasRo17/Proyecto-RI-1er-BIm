Why the Lucky Stiff removed most of his online profile on Aug. 19th, 2009. The author of Why's (poignant) guide to Ruby, Hpricot, and many other projects left behind many confused and upset users. Zed Shaw called it a "dickhead thing to do". John Resig called his work a "Sand Mandala". Do developers have an unwritten obligation to their users? Does their obligation increase with their prestige?
