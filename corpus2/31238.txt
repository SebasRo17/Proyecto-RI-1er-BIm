Given two matrices m1 and m2, e.g.:               m1 = {{a1, b1}, {c1, d1}}     m2 = {{a2, b2}, {c2, d2}}      How can one obtain the following?               {{f[a1, a2], f[b1, b2]}, {f[c1, c2], f[d1 ,d2]}}      I found this solution               MapThread[f, {m1, m2}, 2]      Is there a simpler way?
