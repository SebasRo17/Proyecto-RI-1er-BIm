Let's say you have a list of values that are   1. a   2. b    3. c   4. d   5. e The order on the list might be changed.   1. a    2. c   3. d   4. **b**   5. e How would you detect this change? We needed something like this when we were building a revision history page for our online form building tool. We found a quick and dirty solution but I am looking for a more elegant solution for this. Here is what the revision descriptions look like: ![jotform revisions page](http://i.stack.imgur.com/QKuTx.png) Here is the tough part: **Elements can be removed/added from/to anywhere on the list.** So, the algorithm should be able to ignore those changes.
