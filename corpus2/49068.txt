Given lists $a$ and $b$, which represent multisets, how can I compute the complement $a\setminus b$? I'd like to construct a function `xunion` that returns the symmetric difference of multisets. For example, if $a=\{1, 1, 2, 1, 1, 3\}$ and $b=\{1, 5, 5, 1\}$, then their symmetric difference is $\big((a\cup b)\setminus(a\cap b)\big)\setminus(a\cap b)=(a\setminus b)\cup(b\setminus a)=\{1,1,2,3,5,5\}$.
