Consider the following dataset:               data = Flatten[      Table[{x 10^-9, y 10^-9, x^2 + y^2},{x, -100, 100, 10}, {y, -100,100, 10}]      , 1];      If I try to `ListDensityPlot` this set:               ListDensityPlot[data]      ![Mathematica graphics](http://i.stack.imgur.com/DKNjB.png) it does not plot the function. However, if I do the obvious re-scale of the coordinates:               data2 = Flatten[Table[{x , y , x^2 + y^2}, {x, -100, 100, 10}, {y, -100, 100, 10}], 1];      it has no problem plotting it:               ListDensityPlot[data2]      ![Mathematica graphics](http://i.stack.imgur.com/IsOJV.png) The same problem exists for other plotting methods (`ListPlot3D`, `ListContourPlot`, etc.).
