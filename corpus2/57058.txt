The ordinary differential equations to solve have symbolic parameters $k_1,k_2,k_3,k_4,k_5,k_6 \in \mathbb{R}$. $$ \left\\{ \begin{array}{l} {y_1}'(t)=-{k_1} {y_1}(t)-{k_2} {y_1}(t),\\\ {y_2}'(t)={k_2} {y_1}(t)-{k_3} {y_2}(t),\\\ {y_3}'(t)={k_1} {y_1}(t)+{k_3} {y_2}(t)-{k_4} {y_3}(t),\\\ {y_4}'(t)={k_4} {y_3}(t)-{k_5} {y_2}(t) {y_4}(t)+{k_6} {y_5}(t),\\\ {y_5}'(t)={k_5} {y_2}(t) {y_4}(t)-{k_6} {y_5}(t)\\\ \end{array} \right.$$ The ODE system itself seems simple when all $k_i$ are numerical numbers. How to solve it symbolically ? Is it possible to obtain explicit solutions?
