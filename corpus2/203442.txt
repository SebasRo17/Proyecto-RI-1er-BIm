What is a good practice to name an URL if it contains repetitive letters. For example, `/info/foossite` The two **s** looks kind of odd and if this was access point for a `Web- Service`, this could lead into misspelling. In most programming languages, we have `Camel` notation. However, as URLs are case insensitive, we don't have that luxury here. So, what is the best practice in this regard?
