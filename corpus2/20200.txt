This bug was fixed in version 10.0.0 * * * When `BarChart` layout is `"Stacked"`, setting `BarOrigin -> Top` makes the y-axis tick marks negative. BarChart documentation shows an example - but not "Stacked" - with correct positive y-axis tick marks.
