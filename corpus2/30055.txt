In `ListPlot` and `Interpolation`, the option `InterpolationOrder->0` uses two different conventions. `Interpolation` gives the y-value from the nearest _greater_ x-value. ListLinePlot[{{0,0},{1,1},{2,2}},InterpolationOrder->0,PlotRange->{{-1,3},All}]      ![enter image description here](http://i.stack.imgur.com/Gl11k.png) How can I make `Interpolation` use the `ListPlot` convention?
