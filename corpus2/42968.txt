With this:               d1 = 10; d2 = 4;     mat = RandomReal[{-1, 1}, {d1, d2}];     vec = RandomReal[{-1, 1}, d1];      `LeastSquares[mat, vec]` returns the `x`, that minimizes `Plus @@ ((mat.x-vec)^2)` What is the best way to make mathematica return the `x` that minimizes `Max @@ ((mat.x-vec)^2)` I ended up with this, thank you Daniel ;)               With[{L = Length[First[mat]]},     LinearProgramming[Prepend[ConstantArray[0, L], 1],     Prepend[#, 1] & /@ Riffle[mat, -mat], Riffle[vec, -vec],     Prepend[ConstantArray[-\[Infinity], L], 0]]]
